Covid-19 Vaccination Question Translation Website

Overview: A platform providing translations of Covid-19-related vaccination questions.

Background: To help volunteers at vaccination centers to ease communication with non-english/non-malay speaking patients. This was developed as a collaboration between UBD School of Digital Science and UBD Institute of Health Sciences.

Technologies: Wordpress, Audacity

Features: Mobile-friendly, Text translations for 5 different languages, Playable audio translations.
